LESSON 3 · BANKING

Setting up aBank Account

🎯 BEFORE LISTENING

Prepare students for a bank conversation about opening an account.

Discussion:

"Have you ever opened a bank account? Share your experience."

At the Bank 🏦 NEW ACCOUNT
Conversation between a bank employee and a customer opening a savings account
Bank Employee:Good morning.
Customer:Good morning, sir.
Bank Employee:How can I help you today?
Customer:I'd like to open a new savings account.
Bank Employee:Sure. Do you have your ID card with you?
Customer:Yes, here it is.
Bank Employee:Thank you. Please fill out this form.
Customer:Okay. Do I need to give a photo too?
Bank Employee:Yes. One passport-size photo is required.
Customer:All right. I have one with me.
Bank Employee:Perfect. How much would you like to deposit today?
Customer:I'd like to deposit $500.
Bank Employee:No problem. Please hand me the cash.
Customer:Here you go.
Bank Employee:Thank you. I'll enter the details into the system.
Customer:How long will it take to get my account active?
Bank Employee:Just a few minutes. You'll also receive an ATM card soon.
Customer:Great. Thank you for your help.
Before You Listen
"Have you ever opened a bank account before? Tell us about your experience."
📘 Key Vocabulary
savings account ID card deposit passport-size photo ATM card fill out a form
