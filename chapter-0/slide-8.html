Script

Bank Teller: Hello. How can I help you today?
Customer: Hi. I need to pay my electric bill.
Bank Teller: Sure. Do you have your bill with you?
Customer: Yes. Here it is.
Bank Teller: Thank you. Your total is $45.60.
Bank Teller: Will you pay by cash or card?
Customer: I'll pay by card, please.
Bank Teller: OK. Please insert your card here.
Bank Teller: All done! Here is your receipt.
Customer: Great, thank you!
Bank Teller: You're welcome. Have a nice day!
