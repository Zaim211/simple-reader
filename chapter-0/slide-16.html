💡 Wrap-Up Questions

1️⃣ How can you say you want to open a bank account?
Example: “I’d like to open a new bank account, please.”
2️⃣ What does “Cut your coat according to your cloth” mean?
It means “Spend according to what you have, don’t overspend.”
3️⃣ Wrap up!
Review all new vocabulary: account, deposit, withdrawal, transfer, balance, bank statement, ATM, signature, etc.
4️⃣ What can you say when you want to pay your bill?
Example: “I’d like to pay my electricity bill, please.” or “I want to pay my water bill.”
