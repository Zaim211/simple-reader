New Banking Vocabulary

Click a card to see the definition

Savings Account
A bank account where you keep money safely and earn interest.
Current Account
A bank account for everyday transactions, usually no interest.
Deposit
To put money into your bank account.
Withdraw
To take money out of your account.
Balance
The amount of money currently in your bank account.
Transactions
Records of money going in and out of your account.
ATM
Automated Teller Machine where you can withdraw or deposit money.
Bank Branch
A physical location of the bank you can visit.
